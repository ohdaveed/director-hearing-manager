# Product Guide: Director Hearing Manager

## Initial Concept
The Director Hearing Manager is an enterprise-grade operational tool designed to streamline workflows and processes for the San Francisco Department of Public Health (SFDPH) by automating the compilation, validation, and tracking of Director's Hearing Packets for the Healthy Housing and Vector Control (HHVC) program.

## Primary Objective
**Deepen Automation:** The overarching goal for the next phase is to enhance AI-driven extraction of violations and improve chronology mapping. By refining the Anthropic API ingestion processes, we will further automate the translation of raw field inspection PDFs into structured, legally sound administrative actions.

## Target Audience
The platform remains strictly focused on **Internal Staff Only** (Inspectors, Program Managers, and Admins). By dedicating our UX and feature development to internal workflows, we ensure the tool perfectly matches the day-to-step operational needs of SFDPH HHVC personnel.

## Key Success Metrics
The success of upcoming improvements will be evaluated against:
- **Packet Assembly Time:** A measurable reduction in the hours staff spend compiling PDF packets and assembling exhibits.
- **Ingestion Accuracy:** High confidence and reduced error rates in the AI data extraction pipeline from historical reports.

## Core Value Proposition
**Operational Speed:** Our guiding principle is to accelerate the timeline from initial field inspection to final administrative enforcement. Every feature should be evaluated by its ability to reduce friction and eliminate manual, repetitive tasks, ensuring cases move swiftly through the escalation queue.
